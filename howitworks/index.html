Where We Come From

Our founder was a buyer of sports merchandise for a national chain that has over 8,000 stores. After experiencing both sides of the system, he realized there is a major flaw in the sports industry. It just costs too much to take your family to a game AND buy merchandise to outfit the family.

Retailers charge upwards of 60-70% on top of what they pay for gear, AFTER the manufacturer pays over 20% in royalties to the leagues. It is easy to see how a $1 item becomes a $5 item at retail when you slap a team logo on it. By keeping our margins at a minimum, we can fill our boxes with higher quality products at lower costs to provide our subscribers with an amazing subscription box. We make our subscribers proud to own the products they receive.

When will I be billed for a recurring subscription?

After your initial box is delivered, we process all subscription payments the 25th of each month. After your initial subscription period ends, your account will be charged on the 25th of the month unless cancelled.

When does my Game Day Box Ship?

We have a hard cutoff date each month on the 15th. If the order is received on the 15th and beyond, your box will be included in the next month's shipments.

We always ship starting the 3rd Monday of each month. Due to the volume of orders, it can take us a few days to get out all of the orders. If you have questions about your order, email us at support@gamedaybox.org

Shipping is too expensive for international orders!

We will work with you. We have shipped 3 month subscriptions in one box to international customers to save on the shipping charges for both the subscriber and Game Day Box. Just contact us and we would love to get your favorite gear across the pond!

Do you charge for shipping?

Yes a flat fee of $8.50 to ONLY cover costs of shipping anywhere in the US. International orders will have a higher fee to only cover shipping costs charged by USPS International.

When does Game Day Box ship?

We start shipping all subscriptions on the 3rd Monday of each month (shipping our quantity of orders takes a few days). We start with our newest subscribers. As soon as we ship out from our warehouse, you will receive an automated shipment confirmation to the email you signed up with. We ship all of our subscriptions with USPS.

How are the boxes priced?

Based off the smallest of margins, our pricing reduces our margins even more and rewards those who subscribe for longer periods. Subscribing to a year subscription costs you less than $1 per day! We also do amazing giveaways all year, surprise boxes and special gear that isn't available for non yearly subscribers.

What is your cancellation policy?

All monthly subscriptions (month-to-month or 3 month) can be cancelled at any time. All you have to do is shoot us a quick email at support@gamedaybox.org and we will be happy to help. Cancelling through your profile only cancels your auto-renew, if you need to cancel out the entirety of your subscription please email us at support@gamedaybox.org.
