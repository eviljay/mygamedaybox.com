MEMBERSHIP

What is Game Day Box?

Game Day Box is a monthly subscription box that sends 4-6 items every month of Licensed merchandise from the NFL, NBA, NHL, and MLB. America loves sports. Always have, always will. We are here to help you get ready for the big game. We're a face painted crew that handles all aspects of the business from a suburb of Dallas, Tx. We pride ourselves in our customer service and we are always just a short email away.


How do I join Game Day Box?

Select the "Get in the Game" button on our front page, let us know your t-shirt & hat sizes. We will ask for your favorite teams and after you fill out your shipping & payment info you are in the game!


I just signed up for a subscription, what will I receive?

All High Quality Licensed Sports Merchandise based off YOUR favorite teams. Game Day Box includes 4-6 carefully selected items from our partners. Our selections are based off your favorite teams, our monthly themes and present day trends in the marketplace. We craft our boxes perfectly to ensure you are receiving season appropriate gear every time.


Can I gift Game Day Box?

We think Game Day Box makes an excellent gift for sports fans of all ages. Just click on the Give as a Gift button on our main page and follow the prompts


Do you charge sales tax?

We are based out of Texas, so we only charge sales tax for Texas subscribers. Sorry ya'll.


What's so great about signing up for an entire year?

Besides 12 months of awesomeness from your favorite teams? When you sign up for a year Game Day Box costs you less than $1 per day! We also do amazing giveaways all year, surprise boxes and special gear that isn't available for non yearly subscribers.


When will I be billed for a recurring subscription?

After initial signup, we process all subscription payments the 25th of each month. We ship out by the 3rd Monday of each month and you will receive a tracking confirmation email when your Game Day Box ships. After your initial subscription period, your account will be charged on the 25th of the month unless cancelled.

SHIPPING

Do you charge shipping?

We charge a flat fee of $7.50 for each box we ship regardless of the weight of the box to the US. International orders will have a higher fee to only cover shipping costs charged by USPS International.


When does Game Day Box ship?

We ship all subscriptions on the 3rd Monday of each month. As soon as we ship out from our warehouse, you will receive an automated shipment confirmation to the email you signed up with. We ship all of our subscriptions with USPS.


I live outside of the US, am I eligible to receive a Game Day Box? What if I live on a military base?

Yes, Game Day Box will ship to any country and US military bases across the globe.

ACCOUNT SERVICES

How do I change my billing or shipping information?

Log into your Game Day Box profile and change whatever account information you need changing, can of corn.


Can I change the teams I receive after my subscription starts?

Yes, all bandwagon fans are welcome as well! If you picked the wrong teams at checkout or receive the wrong teams as a gift, we will happily change the teams to any new selections you may have. If you only want to subtract a team or two, that is allowed too!


Can I skip a month or more during my subscription?

Yes! Just sign into your account and select skip. Players go on the DL all the time in sports, its part of the game and we understand. We will be here for you when you are ready to reactivate at any time!


What is your cancellation policy?

All monthly subscriptions (month-to-month or 3 month) can be cancelled at any time. All you have to do is shoot us a quick email at support@gamedaybox.org and we will be happy to help. Cancelling through your profile only cancels your auto-renew, if you need to cancel out the entirety of your subscription please email us at support@mygamedaybox.com.
